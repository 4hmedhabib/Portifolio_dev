A Little Bit About Me!

For more than 5 years now, design has been the central piece of my world. On this fast and mind-blowing journey, I have moved over the years from being a Software Developer to a full time Web Developer and Designer.

At the moment, this journey has bought me to US Mobile where I am a full-time Senior Web Developer. In this position, as with freelance, I am working remotely and I have been for approximately two years.

With the constant goal of pushing developer boundaries and maintaining a high standard for usability & user experience, I have consistently worked with various startups and clients from all around the world and helped them create amazing and engagng digital products.
